How to see the stereogram

Parallel (wall-eye): Relax your gaze as if looking through the screen into the distance until the two dots merge into three.

Cross-eye: Press Cross above, then cross your eyes until the dots merge. The 3D image appears in the centre.

Blur trick: Slide Blur up slightly — soft focus helps your eyes de-converge and lock onto the hidden image.

Zoom: Shrink the image so the dots are closer together — easier for first-timers. Zoom in once locked.
